💬 關鍵句子 💬
"Eat more vegetables."
多吃蔬菜。
"Vegetables are good for you."
蔬菜對你有益處（吃蔬菜對身體好）。
"I don't eat enough of them."
我（蔬菜）吃得不夠多。
"What vegetables are great on sandwiches?"
哪些蔬菜加在三明治上很棒？
"Lettuce, tomatoes, and onions are great on sandwiches."
生菜、番茄和洋蔥加在三明治裡很棒。
"You can cook vegetables such as cabbage or carrots."
你可以烹煮像高麗菜或紅蘿蔔之類的蔬菜。
"Raw vegetables aren't too bad."
生吃蔬菜其實還不錯。
"With good recipes, you can enjoy vegetables."
搭配好的食譜，你就能享受蔬菜的美味。

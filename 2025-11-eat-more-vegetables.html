💬 關鍵句子 💬
"Vegetables are good for you."
蔬菜對你有益處（吃蔬菜對身體好）。
"I don't eat enough of them."
我（蔬菜）吃得不夠多。
"Lettuce, tomatoes, and onions are great on sandwiches."
生菜、番茄和洋蔥加在三明治裡很棒。
"There are many kinds of vegetables."
有很多種蔬菜。
"What kinds of vegetables do you like?"
你喜歡哪種類型的蔬菜？
"I will eat more vegetables."
我會多吃蔬菜的。

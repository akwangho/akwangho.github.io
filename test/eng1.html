B. Grammar & Sentences 文法與句型 (30%, 每題 5 分)

1. How ______ we get to the park?
  1. are
  2. do
  3. can
解答：C (can)
2. We can get there ______ bus.
  1. by
  2. on
  3. in
解答：A (by)
3. Dad can ______ to the bakery by car.
  1. gets
  2. get
  3. getting
解答：B (get，助動詞後接原形動詞)
4. Jello ______ to the post office.
  1. walk
  2. walks
  3. walking
解答：B (walks，第三人稱單數)
5. ______ they get to the zoo by bike?
  1. Can
  2. Are
  3. Do
解答：A (Can)
6. No, we can't. We can get there ______ MRT.
  1. by
  2. take
  3. ride
解答：A (by)

D. Reading Comprehension 閱讀測驗 (30%, 每題 10 分)

Tourist Spots in Sunny Town

Welcome to Sunny Town. Are you ready to have some fun?

Spot 1: Walk to Best Bakery from Sunny Town Train Station. You can get the best pineapple cakes here.
Spot 2: Go to Yellow Park by bike. There are many trees in the park.
Spot 3: Are you hungry? Have lunch at Penny's Restaurant. Don't miss the best pizza in Sunny Town.
1. How can you get to Best Bakery from the train station?
  1. By bus.
  2. By bike.
  3. Walk.
解答：C (Walk)
2. How can you get to Yellow Park?
  1. By bike.
  2. By taxi.
  3. By MRT.
解答：A (By bike)
3. What can you eat at Penny's Restaurant?
  1. Pineapple cakes.
  2. Pizza.
  3. Grapes.
解答：B (Pizza)
